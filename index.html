Artist Bio

I am an illustrator, painter, and color enthusiast from the Treasure Valley. I've lived in Idaho my entire life, but I dream of traveling and perhaps living aborad for a bit. It's also a dream of mine to do illustrations for video games and children's books. Typically I work with pen and acrylic paint to make my pieces. Recently I've started experimenting with watercolor and I really love the way it looks. Hopefully I'll get better at it!
