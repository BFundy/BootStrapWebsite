About

This project was made for my AP Art Portfolio Submission in 2018. I wanted to create a unified collection of illustrations that centered on the idea of holding onto one's passions, particularly as one advances into adulthood. Without personal passions and dreams, life is desaturated and kinda boring.

Inner Child

An overworked office worker lost all her zest a long time ago...

Return to Home

She sits and types all day...

Doing work for someone in charge.

This wasn't what she dreamed of.

Wasn't there something else?

What has she forgotten?

There's something she needs to find.

No, not something...

Someone

It's been a long time since she was a child.

Her younger self had dreams. Great, big, beautiful, colorful dreams.

She thought she had left them behind. But they never left.

Dreams bring zest and wonder. And she found hers in the end.
